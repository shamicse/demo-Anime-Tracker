Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AnimeTracker

General Questions

What is AnimeTracker?

AnimeTracker is a comprehensive platform designed to help anime enthusiasts discover, track, and manage their favorite anime series. You can create personalized watchlists, get recommendations, read reviews, and connect with the anime community.

Is AnimeTracker free to use?

Yes! AnimeTracker is completely free to use. You can create an account, track your anime, create watchlists, and access all features without any cost. We're committed to providing a free service to the anime community.

Do I need to create an account?

While you can browse anime and read content without an account, creating an account unlocks many features including:

  • Personal watchlist management
  • Saving your favorite anime
  • Rating and reviewing anime
  • Getting personalized recommendations
  • Participating in community discussions
How do I search for anime?

You can search for anime using the search bar in the navigation menu. Simply type the anime title, and you'll see instant results. You can also browse our extensive anime list and use filters to find anime by genre, year, rating, and more.

Watchlist & Tracking

How do I add anime to my watchlist?

To add anime to your watchlist, simply navigate to any anime's detail page and click the "Add to Watchlist" button. You can organize your anime into different categories like "Watching", "Completed", "Plan to Watch", "Dropped", and more.

Can I track my progress while watching an anime?

Yes! When you add an anime to your watchlist, you can track your progress by updating the episode count. The progress bar will automatically update to show how much of the series you've completed.

How do I rate an anime?

You can rate anime on a 1-10 scale by visiting the anime's detail page and clicking the "Rate" button. Your ratings help us provide better recommendations and contribute to the community's overall rating system.

What's the difference between Watchlist and Watch Later?

Your Watchlist contains all anime you're currently tracking, including those you're watching, have completed, or plan to watch. "Watch Later" is a specific category for anime you want to save for future viewing but haven't started yet.

Recommendations

How does the recommendation system work?

Our recommendation system uses your watchlist, ratings, and preferences to suggest anime you might enjoy. The more you rate and track anime, the better our recommendations become. You can also manually set preferences like favorite genres, themes, and styles.

Can I customize my recommendations?

Absolutely! Visit the Recommendations page where you can set filters for genres, themes, anime type (TV, Movie, OVA), and more. You can also exclude certain genres or anime you've already watched.

Community & Features

Can I write reviews?

Yes! After watching an anime, you can write detailed reviews to share your thoughts with the community. Reviews help other users discover great anime and avoid those that might not suit their preferences.

How do I participate in community discussions?

Visit our Community page to see featured discussions. You can join conversations, share your opinions, and connect with other anime fans. Make sure you're logged in to participate fully.

Where does the anime data come from?

We aggregate anime information from multiple trusted sources including MyAnimeList (MAL) and AniList APIs. This ensures our database is comprehensive, up-to-date, and accurate.

Technical & Account

Is my data safe and secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All user data is encrypted and stored securely. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information. Your personal watchlists and preferences are private unless you choose to share them.

Can I export my watchlist?

Currently, you can view and manage your watchlist directly on the site. Export functionality may be added in future updates based on user feedback.

What browsers are supported?

AnimeTracker works best on modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend using the latest version of your browser for the best experience. The site is also fully mobile-responsive.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and enter your email address. You'll receive instructions to reset your password. If you continue having issues, please contact our support team.
