About AnimeTracker

Your trusted companion in the world of anime

Welcome to AnimeTracker

AnimeTracker was born from a simple idea: anime fans deserve a better way to discover, track, and share their favorite series. Founded by passionate anime enthusiasts, we've built a platform that combines powerful tracking tools with a vibrant community of like-minded fans.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide anime fans with the best possible platform for discovering new anime, tracking their viewing progress, and connecting with the global anime community. We believe that everyone should have easy access to comprehensive anime information and the tools to manage their personal anime journey.

We're committed to:

  • Providing accurate, up-to-date anime information
  • Creating intuitive tools for tracking and organizing anime
  • Building a welcoming and inclusive community
  • Continuously improving based on user feedback
  • Keeping our platform free and accessible to everyone

What We Offer

AnimeTracker provides a comprehensive suite of features designed to enhance your anime watching experience:

  • Extensive Anime Database: Access information on thousands of anime series, movies, and OVAs from multiple trusted sources
  • Personal Watchlists: Organize your anime into custom categories like "Watching", "Completed", "Plan to Watch", and more
  • Smart Recommendations: Get personalized anime suggestions based on your preferences, ratings, and viewing history
  • Rating System: Rate and review anime to help others discover great shows
  • Community Features: Connect with other fans, participate in discussions, and share your thoughts
  • Blog & Content: Read articles, guides, and insights about the anime world
  • Mobile-Friendly Design: Access your watchlist and explore anime from any device

Our Story

AnimeTracker started as a personal project by anime fans who were frustrated with existing tracking solutions. We wanted something that was both powerful and easy to use, with a modern interface and comprehensive features.

After months of development and testing, we launched AnimeTracker with a simple goal: to create the best anime tracking platform we could imagine. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of anime fans worldwide, and we're constantly working to improve and expand our platform.

We're a small but dedicated team of developers, designers, and anime enthusiasts who believe in making great tools for the community. Every feature we add, every bug we fix, and every improvement we make is driven by our passion for anime and our commitment to our users.
